Books

The Wheel of Time by Robert Jordan & Brandon Sanderson

I started reading The Wheel of Time 7 or so years ago. At the begining of this year I decided to finish them. There are 14 books in total and during the quarantine I listened to 4 of them. The author Robert Jordan died before he could finish; Brandon Sanderson wrote the last 3.

Smoke Bitten by Patrica Briggs

Book #12 is the Mercy Thompson Series, realeased during quarantine. Patrica Briggs is one of my favorite auothors. I started reading these books when they were first published and look forward to each book. Patrica Briggs has a way of writing new story lines that feel like old friends.

Eleanor Oliphant Is Completely Fine by Gail Honeyman

I read this book for a book club. The website that recommended it said it was light hearted... I wouldn't say that it was light and fluffy. It was an interesting read that helped me escape my own head.
